# ⏱️ Tiempos Verbales

## Simple Present (Presente Simple)

**Uso:** Acciones habituales, hechos generales.

**Estructura:**
- Afirmativo: `Subject + verb (base) + complement`
- Negativo: `Subject + do/does + not + verb + complement`
- Interrogativo: `Do/Does + subject + verb + complement?`

**Ejemplos:**
- She **works** every day.
- He **does not** play soccer.
- **Do** they **speak** English?

---

## Past Simple (Pasado Simple)

**Uso:** Acciones completadas en el pasado.

**Estructura:**
- Afirmativo: `Subject + verb (past) + complement`
- Negativo: `Subject + did + not + verb + complement`
- Interrogativo: `Did + subject + verb + complement?`

**Ejemplos:**
- I **studied** last night.
- She **did not** come to class.
- **Did** you **finish** the homework?

---

## Future with "Will"

**Uso:** Predicciones, decisiones espontáneas.

**Estructura:**
- Afirmativo: `Subject + will + verb + complement`
- Negativo: `Subject + will + not + verb + complement`
- Interrogativo: `Will + subject + verb + complement?`

**Ejemplos:**
- I **will travel** next year.
- They **won't** be late.
- **Will** she **call** you?
